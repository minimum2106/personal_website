Projects

FPT-Data-Centric

This competition focuses on enhancing dataset quality by refining data processing and generation techniques for a fixed YOLOv5 model. Instead of just manually cleaning the raw dataset and applying standard augmentations, our team took a smarter approach. We developed a method to automatically identify challenging images and selectively augment them after each training epoch. This strategy significantly improved the model's performance.

ClusterLLM

As part of my master's program, I built upon the original work of the ClusterLLM paper, which investigates how Large Language Models (LLMs) can improve unsupervised learning by refining textual embeddings with an LLM-based evaluator. In this project, I enhanced the authors' approach by developing a custom loss function to replace the original one and fine-tuning hyperparameters to optimize the pipeline.
